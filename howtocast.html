    How to Cast your Fishing Rod

    Fishing may seem daunting at first, but it's an activity with a low skill floor and a high skill ceiling. That means that it's really easy to get into, just hard to get really good. Don't worry about that because fishing depends a lot on luck anyways. Someone can show up with the best gear and the best technique and still not catch anything. So it's best to know the basics and have fun!

    1

    Hold your rod at about waist level, with the reel below the rod. Hold it so that the stem of the reel goes through your fingers for a better grip. The bait should be hanging at least about 5-6 inches. If you find that it's not hanging enough, you can take a look at your reel, flip the metal piece surrounding it (bail) and let out some line.

    2

    Hook your line with your pointer finger, and open the bail. Keep holding the line or else your line will slip out.

    3

    Pull the rod tip back so the tip sweeps over your dominant shoulder, and swing it over. Release the line as you swing.
