    What Bait Should you Use?

    Fake Bait

    Fake bait means plastic bait. These include things like divers, jigs, and plastic worms. These baits are common and easy to get. You can find them at your local fishing store. However, they can sometimes cost a lot, especially if you start amassing your collection. These plastic baits each have a gimmick. Some have tails that wiggle, some have beaks that help it dive, and some shine. But let's just talk about divers for now.

    Live Bait

    Whether you are rigging live baits beneath a bobber or casting out your favorite lure, fishing requires you to pay close attention by watching and waiting for a strike.
